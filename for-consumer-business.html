For Consumer Business Owners

We bring Wall Street quality representation to founders of consumer-focused, middle market businesses at:

  • 1

    Early Growth Stage: 1 Year+, $1 Million+ EBITDA

    We bring in family office and small private equity investors early in the business lifecycle to speed unit growth/production, reduce risk for founders, and maximize founders’ exit by achieving greater scale at maturity.

  • 2

    Expansion Stage: 2+ Years, $2 Million+ EBITDA

    We bring in family office and large private equity investors to speed additional unit growth/production, reduce risk for founders, and maximize founders’ exit by creating a 3-5 year roadmap and partnering with capital to fund exit preparation.

  • 3

    Exit Stage: Complete Sale of Business

    We create a competitive bidding process between existing operators in the space, private equity firms, family offices, and other accredited investors to ensure maximum price is paid for founders’ exit. We are experienced in future growth and new unit development modeling to create sale packages that unlock additional value.
