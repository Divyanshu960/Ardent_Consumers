George Odden

Partner

As an investment banker of over 25 years, George Odden has worked for multinational banks conducting transactions valued in the billions of dollars, as well as having worked in the middle market helping small companies find the right partners to reach business owners’ goals. He started his career as an officer in the United States Navy, serving aboard the USS Tripoli in support of Operation Desert Storm.

Having worked on Wall Street at Dillon Read and UBS, with transactions including mergers and acquisitions as well as debt and equity financing, Mr. Odden also led the M&A efforts of two Fortune 500 companies, Rockwell International and Honeywell Aerospace, working to grow these businesses through acquisition and joint venture.  Mr. Odden left corporate America to return to investment banking, where he now serves small and middle market clients. He co-founded Ardent Consumer to help private owners use capital partners to their advantage as they are growing their businesses and to ultimately sell their businesses at higher valuations. Mr. Odden earned a BA from Cornell University and an MBA from Columbia Business School.

Jordan Geotas

Partner

Jordan Geotas is a mergers and acquisitions advisor, licensed attorney, and a commercial real estate broker.  In his career he has been instrumental in advising businesses as they are contemplating capital transactions, including minority and majority recapitalizations/partnerships and complete exits of businesses. Mr. Geotas co-founded Ardent Consumer to bring Wall Street-quality representation to the middle market business owner.

Mr. Geotas has been a licensed attorney for over 30 years and brings over 20 years of investment banking experience.  Mr. Geotas earned a BA in Political Science and Philosophy from Rutgers College and a Juris Doctorate from Arizona State University. 

Jonathan Chadick

Managing Director

Jonathan Chadick spent the last 5 years of his Investment Banking career focused on multi-unit consumer M&A deals across the car wash, fitness, and wellness spaces. Before co-founding Ardent Consumer, Mr. Chadick served Ardent Advisory Group clients as a Vice President, where he specialized in maximizing market value for businesses with growing membership models and new unit development

Prior to investment banking, Mr. Chadick was on the leadership team at Longboard Asset Management, a commodity trading asset management firm. Mr. Chadick holds a BS in Economics from Wharton Business School at the University of Pennsylvania and an MBA from the University of Notre Dame.
